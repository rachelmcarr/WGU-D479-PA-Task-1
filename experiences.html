Experience Taniti

From beaches to forests and restaurants to arcades, the island of Taniti has something for everyone.

Entertainment

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and to visit the volcano. However, there are other things to do, including:

  • Local History Museum
  • Chartered Fishing Tours
  • Snorkeling
  • Zip-Lining
  • Pubs, clubs, and bars
  • Movie Theater
  • Helicopter Rides
  • Arcades
  • Art Galleries
  • Bowling
  • Golf Course (expected October 2025)

Sightseeing

Most tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, which boasts native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay. Other popular activities include boat or bus tours of the island, hikes in the rainforest, or visits to Tanitiâ€™s active volcano.

Restaurants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Grocery

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.
